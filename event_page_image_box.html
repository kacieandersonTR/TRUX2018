What is Chainsaw Operations 1 - Assistant Sawyer Training?

TR course designed to establish a baseline standard for professional and safe chainsaw operations for Team Rubicon's volunteer disaster responders. Whether you're a seasoned professional or you've never touched a chainsaw, this is the first step toward cutting with TR. You'll learn basic chainsaw operation and maintenance as well as how to limb and buck downed trees.

What can I do with this if I pass?

Graduates of the course are known as Assistant Sawyers, or Sawyer 1s. Sawyers 1 primarily limb and buck trees. Review the Chainsaw Operating Levels and Scope of Practice [link] for full details
